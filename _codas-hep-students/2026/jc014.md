## My research:
Focuses on searching for signs of BSM physics at lepton-hadron colliders through the joint QED and QCD factorization scheme for Deeply Inelastic Scattering.

## My expertise is:
In the joint QED and QCD factorization scheme and calculating contributions from perturbative QCD and QED processes.

## A problem I'm grappling with:
Introducing AI/ML to my work and using it to improve calculations and fill in for data to quantify uncertainties to help when experiments provide new data.

## I've got my eyes on:
AI/ML for the EIC and future colliders.

## I want to know more about:
Developing AI/ML architecture and optimizing its use in physics research.
